Brief history — of our treats

History

Invented in the 18th century in France. Napoleon cake is now one of Russia's favorite pastry. I'll tell you why, because it's freaking delicious (and I'm not even russian). The cake was initially named mille-feuille (french for a "thousand layers") was brought to russia in the early 19th century. It was widely cooked during the festivities after the victory against the French army of Napoleon Bonaparte in 1812.
